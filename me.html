i. Travelling To The Past

Firstly, travelling to the future IS possible. You may ask, How the hell, can you travel to future ? The answer is a bit flowery. So, first you need to travel at 95 - 98% of the speed of light (299, 792, 458 m/s) and then time will start running slowly for you and you will reach the near future. More About This Here. So, for a moving object times slows down. As light's speed is the cosmic speed limit, time stops for time. Thus to travel to past we need to go to negative time. So, we have to travel faster than light. Which seems impossible. Then, come the PARADOXES. These are phenomenas which will remain unexplained, even if travel to the past.
