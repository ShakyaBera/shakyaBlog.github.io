North Bengal #1: Lamhatta - Tinchuley

So, here I am with my first tour blog. Believe me, this tour has been the best one so far.

So without wasting any more precious time of yours, I will start off.

DAY 1

On day one, we woke up at 4:00 am in the morning, because we had our flight at 9:05 am. We got ready boarded our cab and reached the airport. There we performed all the check in processes. After that, we had an hour to wait and nothing to do. So I started window shopping. Like this, I wasted a good amount of time and got ready for boarding our plane. Sadly I wasn't able to take pictures of the plane, or pictures inside the plane. Then we landed on Bagdogra, our driver was waiting for us.
